New

Barcode Scanning for Gem & Diamond Inventory

Scan barcode labels to instantly look up inventory items — the same fast, reliable scanning experience already available with QR codes, now extended to barcodes.

  • Scan barcode labels directly from the global header scanner to locate gem and diamond stock in seconds
  • Works across both Gem and Diamond modules, automatically routing you to the correct inventory detail view
  • Fully compatible with the existing QR code scanning workflow — the scanner seamlessly recognizes both barcode and QR code formats
  • Supports scanning from printed barcode labels generated through the CaratLogic label printing system
Whether your team uses QR codes, barcodes, or a mix of both — the universal scanner now handles it all from a single tap.
02
📦
New

Bulk Import Gems for Purchase Management

Accelerate purchase entry by importing gem inventory in bulk — eliminating repetitive manual data entry for large acquisitions.

  • Upload a structured file to import multiple gem records into a purchase order in a single operation
  • Supports mapping of key gem attributes including stone type, weight, shape, color, clarity, and pricing
  • Validation checks run automatically during import to flag missing or inconsistent data before records are saved
  • Imported gems are linked directly to the purchase record and supplier, maintaining a complete procurement trail

🕐
New

Comprehensive Audit History

Full change-tracking is now available across core modules — giving you complete visibility into who changed what, and when.

  • Every create, update, and delete action is recorded with user, timestamp, and field-level detail
  • Audit history is accessible directly from the detail view of each record — no separate reports required

Audit history is now available across the following modules:

💎 Gem Inventory
👥 Business Contacts
🗎 Invoices
🗎 Quotations
📝 Memo Out
📝 Memo In
🔁 Returns
Audit history strengthens compliance, simplifies dispute resolution, and provides a trustworthy record of every change across your business-critical data.
