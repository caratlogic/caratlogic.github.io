Release Notes

Smarter measurements, expanded certificate uploads, tally audio alerts, and more.

April 4, 2026
01
📏
New

Measurement Ranges (From – To)

Measurements now support a range for each dimension, giving you a more accurate representation of your inventory.

  • Enter a minimum and maximum value for Length, Breadth, and Depth instead of a single number
  • Ranges are displayed clearly across all inventory views, e.g. 5.00 – 7.50
  • Ideal for items with natural size variation or calibrated size ranges
Range-based measurements let you describe your stones more precisely, making it easier to match buyer requirements.
02
📈
Enhanced

Dedicated Length, Breadth & Depth Columns

Gem and diamond inventory tables now show each measurement dimension in its own column for easier viewing and comparison.

  • Separate Length, Breadth, and Depth columns replace the single combined measurement field
  • Quickly scan and compare dimensions across your inventory at a glance
  • Available in both the gem inventory and diamond inventory tables
03
🔧
Enhanced

Sort Inventory by Individual Measurements

You can now sort your inventory by Length, Breadth, or Depth individually, making it easier to find items of a specific size.

  • Click on any measurement column header to sort ascending or descending
  • Quickly locate stones within a specific size range without manual searching
  • Works across both gem and diamond inventory pages
04
📄
Enhanced

Upload Up to 5 Certificate Files

You can now attach up to 5 files per certificate, making it easy to include multiple pages or supporting documents.

  • Upload limit increased from 1 to 5 files per certificate
  • Perfect for multi-page certificates, supplementary reports, or additional lab documentation
05
👥
New

“Referred By” Visible in Contact Details

When viewing a business contact, you can now see who referred them — right from the contact detail panel.

  • The Referred By field is now displayed in the contact detail view
  • Easily track referral relationships without needing to edit the contact
06
🔊
New

Audio Alert for Missing Items During Tally

A clear beep sound now plays when a scanned item is not found during a tally, so you can catch discrepancies immediately.

  • Audible alert plays when a scanned barcode doesn't match any item in the current tally list
  • Also triggers on scan errors, helping you spot misreads quickly
  • No need to keep your eyes on the screen — the sound tells you something needs attention
Audio feedback during tally makes the process faster and less error-prone, especially when scanning large batches.
